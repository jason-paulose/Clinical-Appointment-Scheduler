# Clinical-Appointment-Scheduler

In many healthcare settings, clinical appointment scheduling remains inefficient and error-prone. Clinics often rely on manual processes, phone calls, or disconnected software systems, which leads to operational challenges and negatively impacts patient care. Key issues include overbooking or double-booking that disrupts clinician workflows, high rates of no-shows and last-minute cancellations causing lost revenue, limited patient visibility into available time slots, and poor analytics that prevent clinics from tracking trends or making data-driven decisions. These challenges result in decreased patient satisfaction, wasted staff effort, and inefficient resource utilization. There is a clear need for a streamlined, automated, and user-friendly scheduling solution that improves efficiency, reduces errors, and enhances the overall experience for patients and clinic staff.
